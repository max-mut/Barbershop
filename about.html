Our Story

How a small chair in Kilimani grew into Nairobi's go-to barbershop.

Where it started

SharpCuts opened in 2018 with one chair, one mirror, and a barber who refused to rush a cut. Word spread fast around Kilimani. Within two years the single chair became four, and the small space became a full barbershop with a team trained to the same standard from day one.

We still run on the same idea we started with. A good cut takes the time it takes. No shortcuts, no rushing the last five minutes because someone is waiting.

What we stand for

Precision over speed

Every cut is shaped to the client in the chair, not rushed through to fit the next slot.

Respect for the chair

Clean tools, fresh capes, and a barber who listens before they cut.

Consistency every visit

Same standard whether it's your first cut with us or your fiftieth.
